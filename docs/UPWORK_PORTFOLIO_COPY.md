# Upwork Portfolio Copy

## Project title
Revenue Intelligence & Sales Forecasting Platform | React, FastAPI, Forecasting, Dashboards

## Short description
Built a full-stack analytics platform that transforms sales data into executive dashboards, revenue forecasts, anomaly alerts, and scenario planning outputs.

## Strong bullet points
- Developed a production-style revenue intelligence platform with React and FastAPI
- Implemented multi-model forecasting with Moving Average, Linear Regression, and Random Forest
- Built anomaly detection workflows for unusual revenue spikes and drops
- Added scenario planning for pricing, discount, and demand sensitivity analysis
- Created exportable business reports and CSV upload workflows

## One-line positioning
A polished business intelligence product demo designed to showcase dashboarding, forecasting, and analytics engineering skills.
